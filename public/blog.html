Best Practices for Image to PDF Conversion

Published: January 2025

Get the best results when converting images to PDF by following these tips:

  • Use high-resolution images - Higher resolution means better quality in your PDF
  • Choose the right page size - A4 for documents, Letter for US standards, Auto for photos
  • Consider orientation - Use landscape for wide images, portrait for tall ones
  • Organize before converting - Arrange images in the order you want them to appear
  • Check margins - Add margins for printable documents

JPG vs PNG: Which Format to Use?

Published: January 2025

Understanding when to use JPG vs PNG can help you get better results:

Use JPG for:

  • Photographs and camera images
  • Images with many colors and gradients
  • When file size matters

Use PNG for:

  • Screenshots and digital graphics
  • Images with text
  • Images requiring transparency
  • When quality is more important than file size

Why PDF is the Universal Document Format

Published: January 2025

PDF (Portable Document Format) has become the standard for document sharing because it preserves formatting across all devices and operating systems. Whether you're on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, or Android, a PDF will look exactly the same. This makes it perfect for sharing official documents, forms, contracts, and more.
