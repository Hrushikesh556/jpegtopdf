Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

At ConvertJPGtoPDF.online, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our service.

Key Point: Your files are NEVER uploaded to our servers. All conversion happens directly in your browser.

How Our Converter Works

Our JPG to PDF converter uses client-side JavaScript technology. This means:

  • Your images are processed entirely in your web browser
  • Files never leave your device
  • We cannot see, access, or store your images
  • PDF generation happens locally on your computer/phone

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve our service:

We do NOT collect:
