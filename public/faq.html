Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our JPG to PDF converter.

How do I convert JPG to PDF?

Simply upload your JPG images to our converter, arrange them in your preferred order, select your settings (page size, orientation, margins), and click Convert to PDF. Your PDF will download automatically.

Is this JPG to PDF converter free?

Yes, our converter is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium features locked behind paywalls, and no watermarks on your PDFs.

Is it safe to convert files here?

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. They never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you don't need to create an account or sign up. Our converter works instantly without any registration.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, JPEG, and PNG image formats. These cover the vast majority of images from cameras, phones, and screenshots.

How many images can I convert at once?

You can upload and convert up to 50 images at once into a single PDF document.

Can I reorder the pages?

Yes! After uploading your images, you can drag and drop to reorder them. The order you set will be the page order in your final PDF.

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes, our converter is fully optimized for mobile devices including iPhone, Android phones, and tablets.

What page sizes are available?

We offer A4 (standard international), Letter (US standard), and Auto (fits the image size) page size options.

Can I add margins to my PDF?

Yes, you can choose from three margin options: None (no margins), Small (10px), or Large (20px).
