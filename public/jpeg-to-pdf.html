JPEG vs JPG - What's the Difference?

JPEG and JPG are essentially the same image format. The only difference is the file extension. JPG became popular because older Windows systems only supported 3-character extensions. Our converter handles both formats identically, so you can upload .jpeg or .jpg files without any issues.
